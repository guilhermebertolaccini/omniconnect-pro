# Product Vision — OmniconnectPRO

## What OmniconnectPRO is

OmniconnectPRO is a **Growth Operations platform** that connects marketing, conversations, CRM and AI analytics into a single multi-tenant product.

It helps companies understand:

- where leads come from;
- how leads are handled;
- where conversations are abandoned;
- which sellers are losing opportunities;
- which campaigns generate real commercial intent;
- which opportunities should be recovered.

## Core promise

> Transform conversations into opportunities and opportunities into predictable sales.

## Main layers

The platform is organized into 6 layers:

### 1. Acquisition Layer
Smart Ad Automator (SAA), ads/analytics integrations, campaign quality analysis.

### 2. Conversation Layer
OmniConnect — WhatsApp first, then Instagram, Messenger, email, SMS, RCS.

### 3. Automation Layer
Botify — chatbots, AI agents, lead triage, handoff to humans.

### 4. Sales Layer
Real estate CRM — pipeline, proposals, units, sellers, follow-ups, contracts.

### 5. Intelligence Layer
InsightAI — conversation analytics, lead scoring, objection detection, next best action, lost opportunity detection.

### 6. Executive Layer
Dashboards for CEO, CFO, commercial and marketing — conversion leakage views.

## Ideal customer profile (initial)

- Real estate developers and high-ticket sales operations
- Companies with **large lead volume** (hundreds to thousands of conversations/day)
- Operations dependent on **WhatsApp and sellers**
- Teams with **low CRM data reliability** (manual tabulation gaps)

## Product strategy

OmniconnectPRO starts as a **tech-enabled service**:

- Platform (technology, AI, dashboards, integrations)
- Analyst (human optimizing growth on top of the platform)
- Operational optimization service

Over time, **repeated analyst patterns become standardized product features**. This shapes prioritization: every new feature should either give the analyst leverage or automate a workflow the analyst already does manually.

## Multi-tenant from day one

The product is **always** multi-tenant. Every operational entity belongs to a tenant. There is no "single client" mode.

## Vertical first, broad later

Initial vertical: **real estate**. The CRM and InsightAI prompts are tuned for real estate signals (visits, financing, units, proposals). After this vertical proves itself, expansion to adjacent high-volume verticals (auto, education, healthcare) becomes possible.

## What OmniconnectPRO is NOT

- Not a generic CRM (no Salesforce-replacement ambitions)
- Not a marketing automation tool (no email sequences, drip campaigns)
- Not a help desk / customer service platform
- Not a BI tool (dashboards are operational, not analytical-deep)

When in doubt: if it doesn't help convert conversations into sales, it's out of scope.
